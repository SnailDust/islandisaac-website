Lego Drawing

I made this drawing for one of the open ended drawing promts. This is a drawing of a photo I had taken several years prior of a lego mini figure. Overall I didn't think it turned out too bad, but if I were to do it again I would try to smooth out the shading a little bit.

Dramatic Portait

This drawing was in responce to another open ended promt from ART 101. This time I decided to draw a self portait with dramatic lighing and a discusted look. This was my second to last drawing of the class and really showcased how my proportions and shading had improved compared to my first drawing of the class.

Final Self Portait

This was the final project for ART 101. Another self portait made up of almost a hundred different textures. This was entirely drawn with pencil and took almost a week to complete. This was also drawn on larger eighteen by twenty-four inch paper as opposed to the smaller nine by twelve inch paper, used in most of my other drawings.
